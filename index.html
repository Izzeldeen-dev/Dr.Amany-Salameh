About Dr. Amany Salama

Licensed Psychologist & Counselor

Dr. Amany Salama is a highly experienced psychologist with over 10 years of practice in counseling and psychotherapy. She holds a Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology and is licensed to practice in multiple countries.

Her approach combines evidence-based therapies with compassionate care, tailored to each individual's unique needs. She specializes in cognitive-behavioral therapy, mindfulness techniques, and solution-focused therapy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Online sessions are conducted through a secure, HIPAA-compliant video conferencing platform. Before your scheduled session, you'll receive an email with a link to join the virtual meeting room. All you need is a device with a camera, microphone, and stable internet connection.

We require at least 24 hours notice for appointment cancellations to avoid a cancellation fee. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the scheduled appointment will be charged 50% of the session fee.

At this time, we do not accept insurance directly. However, we can provide you with a detailed receipt (superbill) that you may submit to your insurance company for possible out-of-network reimbursement. We recommend contacting your insurance provider to understand your mental health coverage.

The number of sessions varies depending on your individual needs and goals. Some clients see significant improvement in 6-8 sessions, while others may benefit from longer-term therapy. This will be discussed during your initial consultation, and we'll continually reassess progress together.

Yes, confidentiality is a cornerstone of therapy. What you discuss in sessions will not be shared with anyone without your written permission, except in very specific situations required by law (such as if there is an imminent risk of harm to yourself or others).
